# SkillPro - Technical Training & Vocational Education Management System

Welcome to SkillPro, a robust, full-featured web application designed to streamline the administration, instruction, and student experience for vocational and technical training institutes. From course enrollment to student portal management, SkillPro bridges the gap between aspiring learners and career-ready skills.

## 🚀 Project Overview

SkillPro is an all-in-one institutional platform that manages comprehensive training courses (such as ICT, Plumbing, Welding, and Hotel Management). The platform supports flexible learning models, allowing students to register for either **Online** or **On-site** modes. It features dynamic event tracking, automated notice boards, and a dedicated multi-role portal system to handle daily academic and administrative operations seamlessly.

## 👥 User Roles & Descriptions

### 1. Admin (The Control Center)
- **Course & Batch Management**: Create, update, and manage course structures, fees, certifications, and schedules.
- **User Management**: Oversee and approve Student registrations and Instructor profiles.
- **Bulletin Board Control**: Post and manage campus-wide notices regarding upcoming batches, holidays, seminars, and job fairs.
- **System Oversight**: Monitor inquiries submitted by prospective students and manage the master Event Calendar.

### 2. Instructors (The Educators)
- **Course Delivery**: Access assigned course schedules, student rosters, and curriculum details.
- **Student Tracking**: Monitor student progress, manage grading for exams/workshops, and update course materials.
- **Communication**: Interact with students enrolled in their specific online or on-site modules.

### 3. Students (The Learners)
- **Online Registration**: Browse the course catalog and register for courses by selecting their preferred mode (Online or On-site).
- **Student Portal**: A private dashboard to view enrolled courses, track schedules, view certifications, and check fee statuses.
- **Interactive Calendar**: Access a personalized Event Calendar displaying course start dates, exam schedules, and workshops.
- **Inquiry System**: Submit direct inquiries regarding courses and admissions to the administration.

## 🛠️ Key Features

- **Dynamic Course Catalog**: Detailed insights into course fees, durations, certifications, and instructor bios.
- **Dual-Mode Enrollment**: Seamless checkout/registration flow supporting both virtual and physical attendance configurations.
- **Centralized Notice Board**: Live updates on campus events, seminars, holidays, and exclusive Job Fairs.
- **Interactive Event Calendar**: A visual calendar mapping out crucial academic dates, workshops, and deadlines.
- **Inquiry Management System**: A built-in CRM tool for admins to receive, track, and respond to public inquiries.
